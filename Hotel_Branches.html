Hotel Branches

Earl's Regency are among the best you’ll find when it comes to luxury hotels in Sri Lanka. Found in some of the most picturesque and travelled to destinations in Sri Lanka, each one of our establishments offer a comfortable and feature-rich stay for all of our visitors. Each hotel has a great selection of rooms – from executive rooms to sprawling family suits – you’ll find an abode that caters to every taste with us! All of our establishments are also equipped with the latest facilities to ensure that our guests have the most rewarding time possible during their stay.

Hatton Branch

Located in Hatton, 42 km from Gregory Lake, Earl's Regency provides accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, a fitness centre and a garden.
Each accommodation at the 5-star hotel has mountain views, and guests can enjoy access to a shared lounge and to a terrace.
The accommodation offers room service, a concierge service and organising tours for guests and many more.

Trincomalee Branch

Set in Trincomalee, within 80 metres of Uppuveli beach and 2 km of Trincomalee Railway Station, Earl's Regency in Trincomalee offers accommodation with a private beach area and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive.
The property is around 2 km from Kali Kovil, 3 km from Gokana Temple and 4 km from Maritime and Naval History Museum. The accommodation provides room service and a 24-hour front desk for guests.

Chilaw Branch

Featuring air-conditioned accommodation with a pool with a view, sea view and a terrace, Earl's Regency Hotel in Chilaw provides a great experience. This beachfront property offers access to a balcony and free WiFi.
The bed and breakfast features a flat-screen TV. The kitchen comes with a dishwasher, a toaster and a fridge, as well as coffee machine and kettle.
The daily breakfast offers Full English/Irish, Italian or American options.
