Weddings

Make your day a special one with Kandy’s nicest hotel for weddings, the Earl’s Regency.
Have a royal wedding fit for a king and queen in the majestic town of Kandy. We know how important this day is for you, when two people come hand in hand to integrate their lives, dreams, and hopes. At Earl’s Regency we are all about love, so we’re going to do everything we can to ensure your ‘happily ever after’. Foreign weddings in Sri Lanka have become very popular, and the Earl’s Regency is home to one of the most sought after wedding halls in Kandy for such events. We’re sure to cater to all of your needs and wants, no matter how unique they are.
Venue, catering, decor, attire, bridal party – let us play fairy godmother. Flying in for your wedding? Don’t worry about a thing – communicate your needs with your assigned wedding planner, and we can ensure your end result will be perfection.
The hills of Kandy are blossoming with romance and love is in the air at the Earl’s Regency Hotel.
