Organising Parties or events

Nestled under a large glass roof in daylight, our event room can be adapted to suit your wishes. Two areas seperated by a wooden archway are available, allowing you to create different settings for your parties.
Host your celebration with a flexible venue to choose, all you need to do is invite your friends to enjoy the idyllic setting, allowing our events team to look after your party needs.
A cloakroom is also available at the entrance of the room. Hope you enjoy your dream party with us, and we will try to fulfill your expectations and guarantee to have great celebrations with us
